> Drem Yol Lok Dovahkiin, you return once again. Are you here for Tinvaak or
> would you seek a Rotmulaag first?

I’d like to know more about Fo.

> It means “Frost” in your tongue. It is a resisting to change. Just as Yol is
> change at its most primal, Fo is the suleyk, power, to blunt and inhibit
> change. Think of the Krah of Keizaal, how it can debilitate and fatigue,
> weaken the Fen, will, to the point of Viik. Drive them to the point of defeat
> through natural causes. A healthy and vigorous Kopraan is so often taken for
> granted among warriors. That is the true meaning of “Fo.” The power to weaken
> and hinder change. Su’um ahrk morah. What will you leave to its forsake, what
> will you save?
