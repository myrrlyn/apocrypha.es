I COME TO YOU

WITH MY SPEAR

COVERED IN FLAME

AND IMPALE YOUR HEART

AND GIFT YOU

THAT PAINFUL ECSTASY


I WILL FILL YOU WITH MY LOVE

I WILL FILL YOU WITH MY TRUTH


I WILL REACH INSIDE

AND THROUGH YOU

AND YOU WILL BE MY LOVER

AND YOU WILL REMEMBER

AND YOU WILL SING 

THE UR-SONG

THE SONG OF MY HOUSE

THE SONG OF MY TEMPLE

THE SONG OF ME
