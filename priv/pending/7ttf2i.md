Or eb or,

Or eb or,

Osh ren or,

Ornim dek golzarga or,

Ornim nor ren or,

Or eb or,

Or eb or.

Translation: (By Ulag gro-Bagol)

Stone and Stone,

Stone and Stone,

Iron in Stone,

Orcs gather cherished stone,

Orcs death in stone,

Stone and Stone,

Stone and Stone,
